Volunteer with PA Pride

Help make Pride on the Pier happen! Whether you can give a few hours or the full day (9:00am to 6:00pm) we'd love to hear from you!

What to expect

  • We need coverage all day, but we'll work with you to find a time that works for you.
  • Roles vary: setup, greeting, teardown, and more. Tell us what you're interested in!
  • After you sign up, a coordinator will reach out with next steps.
