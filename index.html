About Us

Celebrating queer joy on the peninsula

PA Pride is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based in Port Angeles, Washington. We exist to build visibility, community, and celebration for lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, intersex, asexual, two-spirit, and allied people across Clallam County and the Olympic Peninsula.

Our flagship event is Pride on the Pier, a free, family-friendly festival held each summer on the Port Angeles waterfront. Donations to PA Pride are fully tax-deductible.

Pride festival

Join us on the pier

Pride on the Pier is a one-day, all-ages festival on the Port Angeles City Pier featuring local performers, community organizations, food, and vendors. It's free to attend and open to everyone.
